Crystals Collector

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amonut of points to you total score.
  3. You win the game be matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you chick on it.
  5. Each time when the gam starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
